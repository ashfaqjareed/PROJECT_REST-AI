Technical FAQ

Do I need to change my WhatsApp number?

No. We can connect to your existing Business number. The AI handles the messages, but you still have full control.

What if I have multiple branches?

Each branch gets its own unique Kitchen Link and its own AI sub-profile for localized menu availability.

Can I see the message history?

Yes. Every interaction is logged and visible in your Owner Dashboard for quality control and customer insights.

What happens to my data?

We use banking-grade encryption. Your customer data is never sold and is stored strictly for your restaurant's use.
