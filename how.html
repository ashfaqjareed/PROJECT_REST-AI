Onboard

From message to meal in 3 simple steps.

Step 1

Customer Messages

The customer scans a QR code or opens your WhatsApp number. They type their order naturally—just like messaging a friend.

Hi! Can I get 2 Chicken Kottu?
Step 2

AI Confirms

Ordera Bot identifies the items, calculates the total (with GST/Service charge), and asks for a table number or delivery address. All in seconds.

Understood! That's 2 Chicken Kottu (Rs. 1700). Please provide your table number.
Step 3

Kitchen Receives

The order pops up on the kitchen display. Staff clicks 'Start' and then 'Ready'. Customer gets a notification automatically.

NEW ORDER #204
2x Chicken Kottu
