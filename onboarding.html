Common Questions

Do my customers need to download anything?
No. They just message your existing WhatsApp Business number. No apps, no websites, no friction.
What if the AI doesn't understand an order?
The AI is trained to ask clarifying questions. If it's still stuck, it will instantly notify your staff to jump in manually.
Can I update my menu myself?
Yes. You can toggle items on/off or change prices instantly from your Owner Dashboard.
